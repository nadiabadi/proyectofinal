The Anti World Tour was the seventh concert tour for Barbadian singer Rihanna to promote her eighth studio album Anti. The tour was announced on November 23 , 2015 through his Twitter account. It began in Jacksonville on March 12, 2016 and will finish in Abu Dhabi , capital of the United Arab Emirates, on November 27, 2016. Rihanna went around the world with this tour with a total of 75 dates spread throughout the world.

The 777 Tour was a promotional tour for Barbadian singer Rihanna, in support of her seventh studio album Unapologetic. The tour was announced in November 2012, and included seven shows, in seven countries, seven consecutive days. It began in Mexico City, and ended in New York. He passed through Toronto, Stockholm, Paris, Berlin and London.

Diamonds World Tour is the fifth concert tour of the Barbadian singer Rihanna, to promote her seventh studio album Unapologetic. The tour was announced on September 6, 2012 during the 2012 MTV Video Music Awards.

The Loud Tour is the fourth concert tour of Barbadian singer Rihanna, in support of her fifth studio album, Loud. The visit was announced on December 7, 2010 and included 95 shows. The tour began in Baltimore, United States, giving a total of 33 shows in North America, and passing through Barbados and Brazil before arriving in Europe , where the singer offered a total of 57 concerts ending on December 22, 2011 at the London O2.
